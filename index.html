what is Sad Data

Sad Data, commonly known as unused useful data, is a situation that arises when important data stays unused for long periods of time. The data feels it's unappreciated and becomes sad. For companies that suffer from Sad Data, symptoms include; ill-informed employees, unable to act pro-actively and no risk-driven decisions.

We at SadData.com are specialized in locating, extracting en caressing the Sad Data in your organization. Your data will flourish in reports, dashboards and machine-learning models. We will keep the amount of Sad Data in your company at a minimum.
